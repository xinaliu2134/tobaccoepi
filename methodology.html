EpiDataKit Methodology Report:
NYTS 2025 Independent Weighted Analysis

Technical Documentation for Data Validation
Author: Dr. Nana Liu, PhD Organization: 深圳循证智康科技有限公司 Date: March 2026

1 Data Source

  • Dataset: NYTS 2025 Public-Use File (nyts2025_puf.sas7bdat)
  • Released: March 4, 2026 by FDA Center for Tobacco Products
  • Sample: 23,630 respondents, grades 6–12
  • Collection period: January 6 – April 4, 2025
  • Format: SAS7BDAT with SAS7BCAT format catalog

4 Analysis Procedures

  • Prevalence estimation: Weighted proportion of “Yes” responses among valid (Yes + No) respondents for each product category.
  • Brand contact rate: Multi-select question (Q13), weighted proportion among current e-cigarette users. Totals exceed 100% as respondents could select multiple brands.
  • Usual brand: Single-select question (Q14), weighted proportion among current e-cigarette users. Totals sum to 100%.
  • School level stratification: Middle school (grades 6–8), High school (grades 9–12).

6 Limitations

  • Public-use file may have suppressed cells for small sample sizes to protect respondent privacy.
  • Unweighted brand estimates may differ from weighted estimates due to differential response patterns across demographic subgroups.
  • Historical comparisons (2020–2024) use published MMWR/CDC weighted estimates, not re-analyzed raw data.
  • Data from 2012, 2015, and 2019–2020 are excluded from trend analysis due to methodology changes in survey design and administration.
