2025 Update — Monitoring the Future Survey (12th Grade) MTF 2025

⚠ Progress Stalled: 2025 Monitoring the Future data shows no statistically significant decline in any youth tobacco product. E-cigarette use among 12th graders: 15.7%. Nicotine pouch use tripled to 4.4%. About 1 in 5 high school seniors reports current tobacco use.

CriterionData RequiredKey Question
