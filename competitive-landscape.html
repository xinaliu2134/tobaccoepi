Market Gaps & Opportunities 市场空白与机会

Key unmet needs in the tobacco data intelligence market that EpiDataKit is uniquely positioned to address.

Gap 1: Epidemiology-Regulatory Bridge空白1: 流行病学-监管桥梁

No existing platform connects public health surveillance data (NYTS, PATH, AE reports) directly to regulatory submission requirements (PMTA, MRTP). Government data is raw; commercial data ignores public health. This leaves PMTA applicants assembling evidence manually from 5+ disconnected sources.

EpiDataKit fills this gap

Gap 2: Cross-Validation Infrastructure空白2: 交叉验证基础设施

No competitor offers systematic cross-validation across multiple tobacco surveillance datasets. EpiDataKit's triple validation (NYTS vs. Altria ALCS vs. CRST/CAsToR) is unprecedented and strengthens the evidentiary weight of findings for FDA review.

Unique to EpiDataKit

Gap 3: Affordable Regulatory Intelligence空白3: 可负担的监管情报

Small and mid-size tobacco manufacturers cannot afford $50K–$200K Euromonitor/Circana subscriptions. They need PMTA-grade data at startup-friendly prices. The $9,900 DaaS report fills a massive pricing gap between free raw data and enterprise platforms.

$9,900 vs. $50K–200K+

Gap 4: Real-Time Youth Surveillance Dashboard空白4: 实时青少年监测仪表盘

With CDC's OSH largely dismantled in 2025, there is an urgent vacuum in accessible tobacco surveillance. NYTS data exists but lacks a public-facing, interactive dashboard. EpiDataKit's 20+ chart dashboard fills this public health communication gap.

Post-OSH opportunity

Gap 5: Brand-Level Epi Analysis空白5: 品牌级流行病学分析

Government platforms report aggregate prevalence but rarely break down by brand (e.g., Geek Bar = 64.1% of youth e-cig market). Commercial platforms track brand share but not health outcomes. Only EpiDataKit bridges brand surveillance with epidemiological context.

Brand + epi = unique

Gap 6: Bilingual Tobacco Intelligence空白6: 双语烟草情报

Chinese tobacco manufacturers (world's largest market) entering US regulatory pathways have zero bilingual data tools. EpiDataKit's EN/CN interface serves a large, underserved audience of Chinese manufacturers navigating FDA CTP processes.

Untapped CN market

Strategic Summary 战略总结: EpiDataKit occupies a "blue ocean" at the intersection of epidemiology and regulatory intelligence — a space where government platforms provide raw data without analysis, and commercial platforms provide market data without public health context. The $9,900 DaaS model captures value from a segment that currently spends $50K–$200K+ assembling equivalent evidence from fragmented sources.

Moat / Defensibility 护城河

  • Domain expertise — PhD tobacco epidemiologist with regulatory science training
  • Cross-validation methodology — not easily replicated without deep data science
  • First-mover — no direct competitor in the regulatory-epi bridge space
  • Newsletter flywheel — research builds audience, audience funds premium reports
  • Bilingual moat — Chinese manufacturer relationships are relationship-driven

Risks & Mitigations 风险与缓解

  • Data dependency — reliant on NYTS/openFDA continuity → mitigated by multi-source approach
  • Scale — solo operator → mitigated by automation (GitHub Actions pipeline)
  • Competition entry — commercial platforms could add epi data → mitigated by cross-validation depth
  • Regulatory changes — FDA CTP priorities shift → mitigated by diversified data sources
  • CDC OSH cuts — data supply risk → creates opportunity as alternative public resource
